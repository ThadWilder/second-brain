Test Scenarios

1
Contractor Sign-up & Profile
Contractor role
  1. Go to the app and tap Post a Job on the landing page
  2. Enter a new email (e.g. yourname+contractor@gmail.com) and password
  3. Complete the Business Profile form (use fake data — license #, insurance #, etc.)
  4. Verify you land on the My Jobs dashboard
  5. Tap Profile tab and confirm your business info is shown
✓ Expected: Profile saved, pre-agreed fee schedule visible, "Add Payment Method" prompt shown.
2
Post a Job (3-Step Flow)
Contractor role
  1. Tap Post Job tab
  2. Step 1 — Scope: enter job title, scope, material supplier, select material status
  3. Step 2 — Logistics: enter address, start window, install price (e.g. 2500), sub payout (e.g. 1200)
  4. Step 3 — Closeout: enter homeowner name, phone, email → tap Post Job
  5. Confirm the success alert and navigate to My Jobs
✓ Expected: Job appears in My Jobs with status "Posted". Homeowner contact info is hidden from the sub's view.
3
Sub Sign-up & Claim a Job
Sub role
  1. Open a new browser tab / incognito window and go to the app
  2. Tap Find Work on the landing page
  3. Sign up with a different email and complete the Pro Profile form
  4. Tap the Job Board tab — you should see the job posted in step 2
  5. Tap the job card → tap Claim Job
✓ Expected: Job moves to "Claimed" status. Contractor's My Jobs shows the updated status.
4
Messaging Between Parties
Both roles
  1. In the sub view: tap Messages tab → tap the claimed job
  2. Send a message: "Ready to start Monday, confirming materials are on-site?"
  3. Switch to contractor view: tap Messages tab → open same job
  4. Verify the sub's message appears → reply back
  5. Switch back to sub view and confirm the reply appeared in real-time
✓ Expected: Messages appear in real-time without refresh. Blue bubbles = your messages, grey = other party.
5
Complete a Job & Leave a Review
Both roles
  1. Sub: in My Jobs, open the claimed job → tap Start Work
  2. Sub: tap Submit Job Complete → enter customer name for sign-off
  3. Contractor: open the job → review and release
  4. Sub: from My Jobs, open the completed job → tap Leave a Review
  5. Select 4–5 stars, select applicable tags (Fast Payment, Clear Communication, etc.), add a comment → Submit
✓ Expected: Review submitted. Contractor's profile shows updated average rating.
6
Sub Directory Search
Contractor role
  1. Tap Find Subs tab
  2. Verify the sub you created appears in the list
  3. Search by the sub's ZIP code — confirm filtering works
  4. Tap View Reviews — confirm the review from step 5 shows with tags
✓ Expected: Sub card shows name, rating, service area, verified badge (if set). Reviews expand inline.

Known Limitations (Web Preview)

💳 Payment entry is mocked on web Stripe's PaymentSheet is native-only. Clicking "Add Payment Method" completes immediately without a card form. Real card entry requires the iOS/Android native build.
🔔 Push notifications don't work in browser Expo push tokens only register on native devices. Notification flows (job claimed, payment failed, etc.) will be silent on web.
📸 Photo upload may behave differently on web Expo ImagePicker uses the browser file picker on web. Uploads should still work but the UI is less polished than native.
📱 Designed for mobile screen sizes The layout is built for phone screens. On desktop browsers, it renders narrow. This is expected for the current phase.
